Connecting to wi-fi

The best and least expensive way to connect with the NURHI iHED administrative site is to use Wi-Fi. You can check to see if there are new courses or an update to the application and send your usage statistics back by accessing a public space that provides Wi-Fi. Just go to Settings on your phone to ensure Wi-Fi is on and that you are connected to an available Wi-Fi connection.

Installing new courses

To install a new course, from the homepage, press on the menu button and go to 'Manage courses'. This will give you a list of the courses to select to download. you will need an active internet connection to get the list of courses and to install them.


[Top]

Updating courses


NUHRI iHED will automatically alert you when there are new updates to your courses available and ready to download. You can also manually check for updates by visiting the manage courses page.


[Top]

Installing/downloading media and videos


When you download a new course, the course download will not include any media or video files. This is to ensure that the course is not too large to download easily, since the videos may be very large. Instead, the media/video files should either be downloaded or copied to your phone SD card separately. NURHI iHED will alert you if any required videos are missing, you can then select to download them. Due to their file size (and to avoid you using up too much of your mobile data connection) videos can only be downloaded when you are on a Wi-Fi connection. If you don't have a Wi-Fi connection, you can download the files directly to your PC, and then copy them to the SD card from there, to do this, follow the instructions from the 'Download via PC' button to guide you through this.


[Top]

Playing media and videos


When there are videos in your course, when you press to play on them, NURHI iHED will use your default or preferred video media player. If you receive a message that the video could not be played, it is likely that you haven't yet downloaded that video.
